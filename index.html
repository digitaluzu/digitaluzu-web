About Us

Shyam Guthikonda

Founder / Lead Developer

Digital Uzu was founded in 2013 by Shyam Guthikonda, after leaving the AAA gaming industry, where he worked as a developer at Kojima Productions (Konami) on the Metal Gear video game series.

Our mission is to build the highest quality software for both our clients and our customers. From apps to games and everything in between, we love building digital things. At Digital Uzu, we build digital.
